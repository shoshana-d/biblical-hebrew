Lesson 2

How is Tanakh Hebrew different from English?

this is the synopsis

Since we will be working from English translations, we need to understand the ways on which Tanakh Hebrew is different from English. The next lesson is about some of the main differences.

  • Order of words is different, in particular, often verb first, adjective after noun (hazeh,hazot,numbers), negative before verb
  • the Hebrew word et which has no English equivalent
  • No Tanakh Hebrew word for of, when 2 nouns together, translated as though "of" between them
  • sentences without verbs
  • hyphens
