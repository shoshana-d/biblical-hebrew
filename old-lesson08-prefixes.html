Prefixes (at the beginning of a word) 
 
 
 
 
 
 

These are the 6 Hebrew "words" which never occur as separate words but are always attached to the beginning of another word. These "words" occur more than 100,000 times in the Tanakh, so it's really important to learn them.

alefbet_consonant_plus_vowel/ha or alefbet_consonant_plus_vowel/ha the
מֶלֶךְ words/w020_melech (a) king   הַמֶּלֶךְwords/w020_hamelech the king  
אִישׁwords/w023_ish (a) man   הָאִישׁwords/w023_xxx the man  

Note that there is no Hebrew word for "a" or "an" so depending on the context a noun such as מֶלֶךְ words/w020_melech can be translated as "king" or "a king".
